## 4. Your Specific Feasibility

| Factor                   | Reality Check                                                                 |
|--------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Capital**              | You’re bootstrapping. Most real sellers preload stock or boost with ads.      |
| **Product Fit**          | Hand-painted/upcycled items = good story, **hard to scale**.                  |
| **Skill Stack**          | Creative + video = great, but **competition is brutal** in that category.     |
| **Operational Risk**     | What if 100K people like your tee and you can only make 2? Refund hell.       |
| **Platform Risk**        | TikTok might get banned/restructured, esp. in U.S.                             |
| **Emotional Load**       | Partner doubt + financial stress = motivation risk.                           |

**Verdict**: Green-light **small-scale pilot**; red-light full-time commitment until you prove sales consistency.

---

## 5. No-Fluff 60-Day Action Plan

1. **Define a single hero SKU**
   - Example: 1 hand-painted oversized tee. 10 pieces max. Cost ≤ $8/unit.

2. **Budget test = $300**
   - $80 for thrift shirts + paint
   - $120 lighting + tripod + basic backdrop
   - $100 TikTok Spark ads to boost content

3. **Content cadence**
   - 5 shorts/week:
     - 2 “making-of”
     - 1 close-up paint process
     - 1 story-time behind the design
     - 1 low-effort trend meme

4. **Conversion funnel**
   - Avoid TikTok Shop early. Use Ko-fi or Shopify Starter to sell directly.

5. **Goal**
   - Sell out 10 pcs in 30 days.
   - Reinvest **only if** ≥30% resale rate achieved.

6. **Kill criteria**
   - If customer acquisition cost (CAC) > 35% of selling price, stop and reassess.

---

## 6. Critical Questions I Need Answered

To build a smarter plan, answer:

1. **Where are you selling to?** Thailand only? Global? Shipping plan?
2. **How much cash can you risk monthly?** Be precise. $100? $500?
3. **Production capacity?** What if 5x more orders hit suddenly?
4. **Your unique angle?** Why *you* vs the 1,000 other painted shirt TikTokers?
5. **Platform mix?** Are you willing to try IG Reels or Shopee Live too?
6. **Business setup?** Do you have a legal structure, bank account, refund method?
7. **Time budget?** How many hours/week can you invest even with 0 results?
8. **Partner role?** Do they control your budget, time, mood? Clarify now.

---

## 7. Final Take

Starting **isn’t dumb**—but assuming TikTok will solve your finances **is**.  
Treat TikTok as a **cheap audience acquisition test**, not a business until proven otherwise.

Run a **controlled pilot**. Don’t scale until:
- You can sell out micro-drops.
- You know CAC, conversion, delivery.

If you can’t tolerate weeks of silence, low views, and slow feedback — better to keep it a hobby.

But if you're hungry, precise, and consistent, TikTok *can* become your growth engine.

**Brutal truth**: If your goal is fast money, TikTok is a **bad bet**.  
If your goal is long-term creative leverage + brand, it’s worth testing.
