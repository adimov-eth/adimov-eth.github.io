TON Stars: Tiered Referral System and Leaderboards

Tiered Referral System

A tiered referral system can incentivize users to bring in more players and reward them for their continued efforts. Here's how it could work:

  • Bronze Tier (1-5 referrals): Basic rewards, such as a small Quark bonus for each referral.
  • Silver Tier (6-15 referrals): Increased Quark rewards and a small Star bonus for each referral.
  • Gold Tier (16-30 referrals): Higher Quark and Star rewards, plus a special boost or in-game item.
  • Platinum Tier (31-50 referrals): Substantial rewards, exclusive items, and a small percentage of the referred players' earnings for a limited time.
  • Diamond Tier (51+ referrals): All previous rewards plus entry into exclusive events, higher voting power in community decisions, and a permanent small percentage of referred players' earnings.

This system encourages continuous referral activity and rewards the most active community builders.

Importance of Leaderboards

Leaderboards are important for several reasons:

  1. Competitive Motivation: They tap into players' competitive nature, encouraging them to play more to climb the rankings.
  2. Social Proof: Top players on leaderboards serve as aspirational figures for others, showcasing what's possible in the game.
  3. Community Engagement: Players often discuss leaderboard rankings, creating more community interaction.
  4. Goal Setting: Leaderboards provide clear, visible goals for players to strive towards.
  5. Retention: The desire to maintain or improve one's ranking can keep players coming back regularly.
  6. Prestige: Being on top of a leaderboard can be a source of pride and status within the game community.

Types of Leaderboards to Consider

  • Daily/Weekly/Monthly Quark earnings
  • Total Star holdings
  • Most missions completed
  • Highest level achieved
  • Most referrals
