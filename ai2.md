## 2. Cold, Hard Analysis

- **Platform fit**: TikTok is still Gen Z’s favorite place to buy based on aesthetics and personality.
- **Spark ads budgeted**: At least you’re not relying purely on algorithm luck.
- **Realistic first-month target (฿1,000–2,000 revenue)**: This keeps pressure low while learning.

### ❌ But Here’s What’s Missing or Misunderstood
| Issue | Brutal Truth |
|-------|--------------|
| Spark Ads Budget | ฿1,500 in Spark ads = ~45k impressions = maybe 5–8 checkouts if your funnel converts at 1%. No guarantee. |
| Inventory Budget | You haven’t clearly accounted for the cost of blank shirts, paint, packaging, or shipping. Those *must* be budgeted separately. |
| “Go viral” as a plan | That’s an outcome, not a strategy. TikTok rewards consistency, ad spend, and live commerce now—not random clips. |
| Posting Frequency | 2–3 clips/week is *below the success threshold*. You need 5–7/week plus 1–2 lives to be relevant in commerce. |
| Product Scope | You’re proposing multiple SKUs. Wrong move. Start with one **hero product** until it sells ≥50 units/month. |
| ROI Assumption | Most sellers breakeven (at best) in month 1. You’ll need ~28 unit sales per drop just to clear your inventory + promo spend. |
